Comfort and Convenience

Private Van and Car Transfers

Traveling to Malapascua Island has never been easier with our private van and car services, offering seamless transfers from Cebu City or the airport directly to Maya Port. Whether you’re arriving in Cebu for the first time or returning for another adventure, our private transportation ensures a hassle-free and comfortable journey.

Why Choose Private Transportation?
Opting for private transportation provides numerous benefits, including:

Convenience: Enjoy door-to-door service from your location in Cebu City or the airport directly to Maya Port. No need to worry about navigating public transportation or waiting for shared rides.

Comfort: Travel in a well-maintained, air-conditioned van or car, ensuring a relaxing and comfortable ride. Our vehicles are equipped with ample space for your luggage and diving gear.

Flexibility: Schedule your transfer at a time that suits you best. Whether you have an early morning flight or a late-night arrival, our services are available to accommodate your needs.

Safety: Our professional drivers are experienced and knowledgeable about the best routes to Maya Port, ensuring a safe and smooth journey.

The Journey
The trip from Cebu City or the airport to Maya Port typically takes around 3-4 hours, depending on traffic conditions. Along the way, you’ll pass through scenic landscapes and bustling towns, giving you a glimpse of the local culture and environment.

Booking Your Transfer
Booking your private transfer is simple and straightforward. You can reserve your ride in advance through our website or contact our customer service team for assistance. We offer competitive rates and various vehicle options to suit your group size and preferences.

Additional Services
In addition to private transfers, we also offer:


Customized Tours: Explore Cebu and its surrounding areas with our tailored tour packages. Visit popular attractions, hidden gems, and cultural landmarks with a knowledgeable guide.
Diving Transfers: For diving enthusiasts, we provide specialized transfers that include space for your diving equipment and gear, ensuring everything arrives safely at your destination.

Start your Malapascua adventure with ease and comfort by choosing our private van and car services from Cebu City or the airport to Maya Port. Enjoy the convenience, flexibility, and safety of our professional transportation services, making your journey as enjoyable as your destination.
