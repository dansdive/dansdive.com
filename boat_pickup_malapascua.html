Smooth Sailing

Private Boat Transfers

Embark on a seamless journey to the stunning Malapascua Island with our exclusive private boat transfer service from Maya Port. Designed for comfort, convenience, and efficiency, our private boat transfers ensure you start your island adventure on the right note.

Why Choose Private Boat Transfers?
Opting for a private boat transfer offers numerous advantages:

Exclusivity: Enjoy the privacy and comfort of having the boat all to yourself or your group. No need to share the space with other passengers, allowing for a more personalized experience.
Flexibility: Schedule your transfer at a time that suits you best. Whether you’re arriving early in the morning or late in the afternoon, our private boats are available to accommodate your schedule.
Speed and Efficiency: Private boats can often make the journey faster than public ferries, getting you to Malapascua Island quickly and efficiently.
Comfort: Our boats are well-maintained and equipped with comfortable seating, ensuring a pleasant ride across the waters.

The Journey
The boat ride from Maya Port to Malapascua Island typically takes around 30 to 45 minutes, depending on sea conditions. As you glide across the clear blue waters, you’ll be treated to breathtaking views of the surrounding seascape, making the journey as enjoyable as the destination.

Booking Your Transfer
Booking your private boat transfer is simple and hassle-free. You can reserve your ride in advance through our website or contact our customer service team for assistance. We offer competitive rates and various boat options to suit your group size and preferences.

Additional Services
In addition to private boat transfers, we also offer:


Island Hopping Tours: Explore the nearby islands and hidden gems with our customized island hopping tours. Discover pristine beaches, vibrant coral reefs, and unique marine life.
Diving Transfers: For diving enthusiasts, we provide specialized transfers that include space for your diving equipment and gear, ensuring everything arrives safely at your destination.

Experience the ultimate convenience and comfort with our private boat transfer service from Maya Port to Malapascua Island. Enjoy the flexibility, exclusivity, and efficiency of our professional transportation services, making your journey as memorable as your stay on the island.
