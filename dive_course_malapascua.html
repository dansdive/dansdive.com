Scuba Diving Courses

Our scuba diving courses cater to all levels of divers, from beginners to advanced. The Discover Scuba Diving program offers a taste of the underwater world under the guidance of a certified instructor, perfect for those new to diving. For those ready to take the plunge, the Open Water Diver Course provides a comprehensive foundation in diving skills and knowledge, preparing you for safe and confident underwater exploration. The Advanced Open Water Diver Course further enhances your diving abilities, allowing you to gain experience in various environments and specialties. Discover Scuba Diving

Remember that a Discover Scuba Diving experience is an introductory program designed to give you a taste of scuba diving under the guidance of a certified instructor. If you decide to pursue scuba diving further, you can enroll in a certification. course to become a certified diver.This course covers basic theory, equipment familiarization, breathing techniques, basic underwater communication, buoyancy control, marine life awareness, safety guidelines, and basic dive planning.

Open Water Diver Course

The Open Water Diver course provides a comprehensive foundation in scuba diving knowledge and skills, preparing you to explore the underwater world safely and confidently. It's the first step toward becoming a certified scuba diver.This comprehensive course includes diving theory, equipment knowledge, essential diving skills, underwater communication, dive planning, open water dives, marine environment awareness, and certification.

Advanced Open Water Diver Course

The Advanced Open Water Diver course is designed to expand your diving knowledge and skills and increase your confidence as a diver. It's a great way to gain experience in different diving environments and specialties while under the supervision of a certified instructor. This advanced course focuses on enhancing diving skills, deep diving, underwater navigation, underwater naturalist, night diving, peak performance buoyancy, wreck diving, drift diving, and certification.

Scuba Review or Scuba Refresher

A refresh diving program is an opportunity for certified divers to review and reinforce their scuba diving skills, knowledge and confidence before returning to diving activities. It's an essential step for divers who have taken a break from diving or feel a bit rusty and want to ensure they're prepared for their next underwater adventure.This refresher course includes equipment familiarization, safety procedures, buoyancy control, underwater communication, dive planning and execution, navigation skills, marine environment awareness, and confidence building.
