Discover Scuba Diving

Remember that a Discover Scuba Diving experience is an introductory program designed to give you a taste of scuba diving under the guidance of a certified instructor. If you decide to pursue scuba diving further, you can enroll in a certification course to become a certified diver.




Open Water Diver Course

The Open Water Diver course provides a comprehensive foundation in scuba diving knowledge and skills, preparing you to explore the underwater world safely and confidently. It's the first step toward becoming a certified scuba diver and opens up opportunities for further training and diving adventures.




Advanced Open Water Diver Course

The Advanced Open Water Diver course is designed to expand your diving knowledge and skills and increase your confidence as a diver. It's a great way to gain experience in different diving environments and specialties while under the supervision of a certified instructor.




Scuba Review or Scuba Refresher

A refresh diving program is an opportunity for certified divers to review and reinforce their scuba diving skills, knowledge and confidence before returning to diving activities. It's an essential step for divers who have taken a break from diving or feel a bit rusty and want to ensure they're prepared for their next underwater adventure.
